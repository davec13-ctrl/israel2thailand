Our Story

From Tel Aviv to
Chiang Mai — and Beyond

It started with a dream and a one-way ticket. We were a regular Israeli family — two parents, two kids, a busy apartment, and a nagging feeling that life could look completely different.

In 2018 we packed everything, said goodbye to the familiar, and landed in Thailand. The first year was full of surprises — cultural shifts, visa bureaucracy, school hunting, and learning where to buy hummus. But also: warm smiles, golden temples, and a pace of life we didn't know we were missing.

Today we live our best life here — and we've made it our mission to help other Israeli families do the same, with far less guesswork.

✈️
2018 — The Big Move Left Israel, arrived in Chiang Mai with two suitcases and a lot of hope.
📚
